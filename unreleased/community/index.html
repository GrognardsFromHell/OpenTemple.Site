Join the OpenTemple community

OpenTemple is an open source project that anyone in the community can use, improve, and enjoy. We'd love you to join us! Here's a few ways to find out what's happening and get involved.

Learn and Connect

Using or want to use OpenTemple? Find out more here:

  • Forums: Discussion and help from your fellow users
  • Mail us!: If you need support or just want to drop us a note, you can write us an E-Mail!

Develop and Contribute

If you want to get more involved by contributing to OpenTemple, join us here:

  • GitHub: Development takes place here!
